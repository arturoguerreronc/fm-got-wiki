The Great Houses of Westeros

Noble Families of the Seven Kingdoms

The noble houses of Westeros have played pivotal roles in shaping the history and politics of the Seven Kingdoms. Each house has its own sigil, words, and seat of power, representing their unique identity and heritage.

House Stark

"Winter Is Coming"

Seat: Winterfell

Region: The North

The Starks ruled as Kings in the North for thousands of years before bending the knee to the Targaryens. Known for their honor and resilience, they are one of the oldest houses in Westeros, descended from the First Men.

Key Members:

  • Eddard "Ned" Stark
  • Catelyn Stark
  • Robb Stark
  • Sansa Stark
  • Arya Stark
  • Bran Stark
  • Jon Snow

House Lannister

"Hear Me Roar!"

Seat: Casterly Rock

Region: The Westerlands

The Lannisters are known for their wealth, derived from the gold mines of the Westerlands. Their unofficial motto, "A Lannister always pays his debts," reflects both their financial power and their tendency to repay slights.

Key Members:

  • Tywin Lannister
  • Cersei Lannister
  • Jaime Lannister
  • Tyrion Lannister
  • Joffrey Baratheon
  • Myrcella Baratheon
  • Tommen Baratheon

House Targaryen

"Fire and Blood"

Seat: Dragonstone (formerly King's Landing)

Region: The Crownlands

The Targaryens ruled the Seven Kingdoms for nearly 300 years. Of Valyrian descent, they are known for their silver hair, purple eyes, and their bond with dragons. The family is prone to madness, believed to be caused by their practice of incestuous marriages.

Key Members:

  • Aerys II "The Mad King" Targaryen
  • Rhaegar Targaryen
  • Viserys Targaryen
  • Daenerys Targaryen
  • Jon Snow (Aegon Targaryen)

House Baratheon

"Ours is the Fury"

Seat: Storm's End

Region: The Stormlands

House Baratheon is the youngest of the great houses, founded by Orys Baratheon, rumored to be the half-brother of Aegon the Conqueror. Robert Baratheon's rebellion ended the Targaryen dynasty, establishing the Baratheon line as the new royal family.

Key Members:

  • Robert Baratheon
  • Stannis Baratheon
  • Renly Baratheon
  • Shireen Baratheon
  • Gendry (bastard)

House Tyrell

"Growing Strong"

Seat: Highgarden

Region: The Reach

The Tyrells rose to power as stewards to House Gardener, the Kings of the Reach. After the Gardeners were wiped out during Aegon's Conquest, the Tyrells surrendered Highgarden and were rewarded with lordship of the Reach. They control the most fertile region of Westeros.

Key Members:

  • Mace Tyrell
  • Olenna Tyrell
  • Loras Tyrell
  • Margaery Tyrell

House Greyjoy

"We Do Not Sow"

Seat: Pyke

Region: The Iron Islands

The Greyjoys rule over the Iron Islands, a harsh archipelago off the western coast of Westeros. The ironborn are known for their seafaring prowess and their tradition of reaving and raiding, following the Old Way and worshipping the Drowned God.

Key Members:

  • Balon Greyjoy
  • Euron Greyjoy
  • Victarion Greyjoy
  • Theon Greyjoy
  • Yara (Asha) Greyjoy
